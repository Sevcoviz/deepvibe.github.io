Key Components

  • Dual Input Fusion: Combines 10,000-dim interaction vector with 12-dim audio features (acousticness, energy, valence, etc.)
  • SELU Activation: Self-normalizing activation ensures stable training and prevents vanishing gradients
  • Reparameterization Trick: Enables backpropagation through stochastic sampling (z = μ + σ · ε)
  • Gradient Clipping: Log-variance clamped to [-10, 10] for numerical stability

How It Works

  1. 1
    Your streaming history is converted into a sparse interaction vector showing play counts per track
  2. 2
    Weighted audio features are computed from your listening patterns
  3. 3
    The encoder learns a 256-dimensional "taste signature" in latent space
  4. 4
    The decoder reconstructs predicted preferences, which are used to find similar tracks via cosine similarity

The Spotify Manual

Follow these steps to export your raw data package.

STEP 01

Access Privacy Settings

Log into your account on spotify.com/account/privacy. Note: This cannot be done via the mobile or desktop app.

STEP 02

Request "Extended Streaming History"

Scroll down to "Download your data". Check the box for Extended streaming history. This provides the JSON files needed for our AI to be accurate.

STEP 03

Confirm & Wait

Spotify will send a confirmation email. Click the link inside. It takes 5-30 days for Spotify to prepare your package (they will email you once it's ready).

STEP 04

Extract and Upload

You will receive a .zip file. Drag it into DeepVibe!
